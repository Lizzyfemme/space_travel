About Urania

Did you ever dream of being an astronaut? Now you can. Urania is the first resort in orbit around the earth. We offer breathtaking views and a new perspective on life. If you're feeling adventurous, our vacations packages offer some unique experiences like, a space walk or a moon trip.

business goals

Increase brand awareness by using instagram, tik-tok and science fiction conventions. Get 1,000,000 visitors to the website by the end of the year. Covert 1% of webite traffic to sales.

product goals

Have users sign up and create an account. Display information about the resort and its features. Allow users to view scheduled flights and purchase vacation packages.

user goals

Our guests dreamed of being to be astronauts as kids and love science fiction. They want thrilling once in a lifetime trip to space.

risks

Shuttle trips must be 100% full in order to be profitable. A shuttle or resort could explode or be damaged and kill some or all guests.
